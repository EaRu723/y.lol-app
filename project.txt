DESIGN NOTES:
- Color Palette:
  Light Mode:
  * Background: #F5F2E9 (parchment)
  * Text: #2C2C2C (varying opacities)
  * Accent: #E4D5B7 (warm beige)
  
  Dark Mode:
  * Background: #1C1C1E (dark background)
  * Text: #F5F2E9 (light parchment)
  * Accent: #B8A179 (darker warm accent)

- Typography:
  * Serif font family
  * User messages: Regular weight, 16pt, 90% opacity
  * AI messages: Light weight, 14pt, 75% opacity
  * Metadata: Light weight, 8pt, 30% opacity

- Design Philosophy:
  * Minimal, magical interactions
  * Document-like editing experience
  * Contextual input placement
  * Subtle and purposeful animations
  * Natural and fluid movements
  * Enhanced feedback during AI interactions
